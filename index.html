A Love Missed in College

A story from NOIRVA

I saw her every day near the canteen...
Her laughter floated in the air, and yet,
she never knew I even existed.

I had words trapped in my chest, waiting
for courage that never came.
Days turned into semesters,
and she remained a beautiful mystery in my story.

Time didn't wait. Neither did she.
Life moved forward, but my silence stayed behind.
Now years have passed...
And sometimes I walk near a café and smile.

Not because I forgot her,
but because I remember loving someone silently.
Because some stories aren’t meant to be told aloud—
they’re meant to be lived once, and cherished forever.

— A memory, a pain, a lesson... from someone who felt deeply.
